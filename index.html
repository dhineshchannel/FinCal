💡 Where to Park Your Emergency Fund

• Liquid Mutual Funds — instant redemption, ~6–7% returns
• High-yield Savings A/C — 3.5–7%, zero lock-in (Kotak, DBS, IDFC First)
• Ultra Short / Overnight Funds — very low risk, T+1 redemption
• Sweep-in FD — auto-breaks on withdrawal, earns FD rate

❌ Never park emergency funds in equity, crypto, or lock-in products.
