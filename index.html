\ud83d\udca1 Where to Park Emergency Funds

\u2022 Liquid Mutual Funds \u2014 instant redemption, ~6\u20137% returns (better than savings a/c)
\u2022 High-yield Savings A/C \u2014 3.5\u20137% with zero lock-in (Kotak, DBS, IDFC First)
\u2022 Ultra Short-term / Overnight Funds \u2014 very low risk, T+1 redemption
\u2022 Sweep-in FD \u2014 auto-breaks on withdrawal, earns FD rate

\u274c Never park emergency funds in equity, crypto, or lock-in products.
